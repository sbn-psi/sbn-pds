CMOR Meteoroid Stream Survey

A seven-year radar survey of meteor showers has been carried out with the Canadian Meteor Orbit Radar (CMOR) from 2002-2008 (Brown et al. 2008, 2010). This survey resulted in the unambiguous detection and orbital characterization of 109 major and minor meteor showers. This data set includes a list of the detected meteor showers along with their orbital parameters.

Brown, P., R.J. Weryk, D.K. Wong, and J. Jones, A meteoroid stream survey using the Canadian Meteor Orbit Radar I. Methodology and radiant catalog, Icarus 195, 317-339, 2008.

Brown, P., D.K. Wong, R.J. Weryk, and P. Wiegert, A meteoroid stream survey using the Canadian Meteor Orbit Radar II: Identification of minor showers using a 3D wavelet transform. Icarus 207, 66-81, 2010.
