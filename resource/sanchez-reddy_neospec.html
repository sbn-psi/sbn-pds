Near-infrared spectra of small NEOs by Sanchez and Reddy

This dataset includes near-infrared (NIR) spectra (~0.7-2.5 microns) of 82 small near-Earth objects with absolute visual magnitudes H>20 (mean diameter of 126 m). All spectra were obtained with the IRTF and the SpeX instrument over the course of ~7 years. These NIR spectra are published in Sanchez et al. 2024.

Release Date: 2025-04-15
