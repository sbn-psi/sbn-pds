Cassini High Rate Detector (HRD)

The High Rate Detector (HRD) from the University of Chicago is an independent part of the CDA instrument on the Cassini Orbiter that measures the dust flux and particle mass distribution of dust particles hitting the HRD detectors. This data set includes all data from the HRD through the end of the mission.

The Cassini CDA data are also available, and can be accessed at this link.
