Earth-Asteroid Close Approaches

This table presents a list of predicted close approaches to Earth by Potentially Hazardous Asteroids (PHAs). To be classified as a PHA, an asteroid must physically approach within 0.2AU of Earth and have an absolute magnitude brighter than 22.0.
