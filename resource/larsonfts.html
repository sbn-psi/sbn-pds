Larson FTS Asteroid Spectra

This data set contains 107 asteroid spectra obtained between the years 1975 and 1982 inclusive, with the infrared fourier transform spectrometer (FTS) of H. P. Larson. These spectra were taken by various observers and at various telescopes.
