Hayabusa2 MASCOT Bus Bundle

The Mascot Bus bundle is the set of lander housekeeping records for each MASCOT Lander observation set. The housekeeping readings only pertain to the lander bus and should not be confused with any instrument specific housekeeping reported in other MASCOT bundles. The Bus bundle also includes a document describing the housekeeping data included, and the operations document describing the operational life of the MASCOT lander.
