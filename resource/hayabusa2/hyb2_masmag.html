Hayabusa2 MASCOT MAG Bundle

This PDS4 bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the Hayabusa2 MASCOT Magnetometer through the end of the (162173) Ryugu encounter. The instrument is a vector-compensated three-axis fluxgate magnetometer.
