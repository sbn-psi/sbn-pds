Hayabusa2 MASCOT MARA Bundle

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the Hayabusa2 MASCOT Radiometer (MARA) through the end of the (162173) Ryugu encounter. MARA is a multispectral radiometer which measures net radiative flux in six wavelength bands.

Status: Archived
Release date: Aug. 30, 2023
