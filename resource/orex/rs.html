OSIRIS-REx Raw Radio Science Bundle

This bundle collects Tracking and Navigation Files (TNF), Ionospheric Data Files (ION), Troposphere Data Files (TRO), and Small Forces Files (SFF) acquired by the Origins, Spectral Interpretation, Resource Identification, Security, Regolith Explorer (OSIRIS-REx) spacecraft and the Deep Space Network (DSN) on Earth. Relevant mission-independent calibration files (TRO, WEA, EOP) are referenced here as secondary collections.

Release date: Nov. 7, 2022

Status: Archived
