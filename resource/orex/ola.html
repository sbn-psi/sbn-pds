OSIRIS-REx OLA Bundle

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Laser Altimeter (OLA). OLA is used for the topographic characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu.

The bundle now includes all data through Nov. 17, 2019.

Release dates:

  • Initial release Nov. 7, 2019: Data through Feb. 17, 2019
  • Release on Dec. 12, 2019: Data through May 17, 2019
  • Release on Feb. 25, 2020: Data through Aug. 17, 2019
  • (No data taken during Aug. 17 - Nov. 17, 2019)
  • (OLA data for Nov. 18, 2019 - Feb. 17, 2020 expected soon.)
