OVIRS v11.0 Bundle

This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Visible and InfraRed Spectrometer (OVIRS). OVIRS is used for the spectral characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu. This bundle contains all the data through the end of the Bennu encounter.

Status: Certified
Release date: Oct. 24, 2022
