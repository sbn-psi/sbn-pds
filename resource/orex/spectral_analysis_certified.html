OSIRIS-REx Spectral Analysis Bundle

This bundle collects all the derived spectral data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Visible and InfraRed Spectrometer (OVIRS) and the OSIRIS-REx Thermal Emission Spectrometer (OTES). These products include resampled and reflectance OVIRS spectra, and temperature/emissivity OTES data products. Additionally, both visible and near infrared (vnir) and thermal infrared (tir) map products are collected.

The Spectral Analysis bundle is certified for immediate release pending final PDS4 validation. All review liens have been resolved.
