Mechanical Properties of Oued Chebeika 002

This archive provides nanoindentation measurements, associated wavelength dispersive spectroscopy point analyses,wavelength dispersive spectroscopy maps, and one microindentation grid for the Oued Chebeika 002 carbonaceous chondrite meteorite, together with nanoindentation data for four terrestrial phyllosilicate standards.
