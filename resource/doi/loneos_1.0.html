Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains over 200,000 2.88°x1.44° original and a ugmented FITS images from the Lowell Observatory Near Earth Objects Survey (LONEOS) using the 0.6m f/1.9 LONEOS Schmidt telescope and the Lowell Observatory Imaging Software (LOIS) versions 3.2.0.beta and 4.2.0 between 2003-08-31 and 2008-03-01. The original images are from Hard Disk Drives (HDDs) obtained from the Lowell Observatory.
