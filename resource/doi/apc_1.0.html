Overview

  • Abstract: The Asteroid Photometric Catalog (3rd update), Lagerkvist et al. 1993 [LAGERKVISTETAL1993], is a compilation of all asteroid lightcurve photometry published up to and including the year 1992. The dataset includes the lightcurves in digital form and a table of references to all original publications
