Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains raw radio science tracking data and associated calibration files from the NEAR mission. There are six product types: Orbit Determination Files (ODF), Earth Orientation Parameters (EOP), Troposphere (TRO), Ionosphere (ION), Numerical Model (nmlmodl), and solution pages (Paramsum).
