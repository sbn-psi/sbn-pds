Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle collects all the derived spectral data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Visible and InfraRed Spectrometer (OVIRS) and the OSIRIS-REx Thermal Emission Spectrometer (OTES). These products include resampled and reflectance OVIRS spectra, and temperature/emissivity OTES data products. Additionally, both visible and near infrared (vnir) and thermal infrared (tir) map products are collected.
