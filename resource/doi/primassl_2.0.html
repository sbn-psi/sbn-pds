Overview

  • Abstract: PRIMASS-L is a spectral library that contains the results of the PRIMitive Asteroids Spectroscopic Survey (PRIMASS). As of the end of 2023 this library contains 438 visible and 264 near-infrared spectra of primitive asteroids from 10 families and two groups that had been sparsely studied before. PRIMASS-L contains spectra from a variety of ground-based facilities.
