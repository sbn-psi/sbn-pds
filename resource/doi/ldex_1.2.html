Overview

  • Abstract: LDEX is an instrument on the Lunar Atmosphere and Dust Environment Explorer (LADEE) spacecraft designed to study the lunar dust environment by measuring the physical properties of the particles impacting the instrument target during the mission. This archive includes reduced, calibrated, and derived LDEX data from the full LADEE mission, including the extended mission, covering the period Nov. 21, 2013 through April 18, 2014. Housekeeping data are also included.
