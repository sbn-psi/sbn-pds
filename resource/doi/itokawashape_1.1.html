Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains the shape model of 25143 Itokawa derived by Robert Gaskell from Hayabusa AMICA images. The model is provided in the implicitly connected quadrilateral (ICQ) format in four levels of resolution. This version of the model was prepared on August 29, 2007. Vertex-facet versions of the models are also provided.
