Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle collects all the operational data products produced by the OSIRIS-REx Laser Altimeter (OLA). OLA is used for the topographic characterization of the surface of (101955) Bennu. This version is updated with collectins for the V20 and V21 OLA L2A strip adjusted products. The V20 products are archived for historical purposes and are based on version 1 of the OLA dta products. V21 products are the best and final products and should be used for scientific ananalysis. V21 products are based on version 2 "_v2" OLA products.
