Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains radiometrically corrected point spectra of asteroid 951 Gaspra as acquired by the Galileo spacecraft Near Infrared Mapping Spectrometer (NIMS) on October 29, 1991. The calibrated spectra consist of radiance measurements for wavelengths between 0.7 - 5.2 micrometers.
