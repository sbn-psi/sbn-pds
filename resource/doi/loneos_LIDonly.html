Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains data and documentation from the Lowell Observatory Near Earth Object Survey (LONEOS) and Near-Earth Asteroid Photometric Survey (NEAPS). The goal of the LONEOS survey, starting on 1998/02/26, was to discover near-Earth asteroids by observing regions of the sky primarily near the ecliptic and, starting on 2010/01/04, to obtain rotational lightcurves for Near-Earth Asteroids (NEAPS). Note: These, and all other start and end dates given in any V2.0 document, refer to the earliest and latest images recovered from those we received from the Lowell Observatory. For reasons given in other V2.0 documents there are many nights on which images were obtained but that have been lost or that proved to be unusable.
