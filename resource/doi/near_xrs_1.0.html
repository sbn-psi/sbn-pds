Overview

  • Abstract: The NEAR X-Ray Spectrometer (XRS) is part of the X-Ray/Gamma-Ray Spectrometer (XGRS) instrument package on the NEAR spacecraft. This bundle contains all of the raw and calibrated XRS instrument data from the NEAR mission, plus derived results.
