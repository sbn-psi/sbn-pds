Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains orbital and physical properties for well-observed or suspected binary/multiple minor planets including the Pluto system, compiled from the published literature. In total 347 companions in 329 systems are included. This data set is complete for binary/multiple components reported through 31 March 2018.
