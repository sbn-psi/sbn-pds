Overview

  • Abstract: The bundle contains derived models of Eros gravity and topography from radio science tracking. There are four product types: Landmark, Image, Spherical Harmonics ASCII Data Records (SHADR), and the Spherical Harmonics Binary Data Records (SHBDR).
