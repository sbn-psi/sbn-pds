    Overview

    • Abstract: This bundle provides taxonomical information from Dark Energy Survey (DES) data for 10213 asteroids for which gri slope and i-z colors are available, taxonomical complex information for 58118 asteroids with DES colors g-r, g-i, and a list of 409 new possible V-type objects.
