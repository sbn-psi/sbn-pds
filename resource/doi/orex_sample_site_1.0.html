Overview

  • Abstract: This collection contains detailed catalogs created during the Sample Site Selection Phase of the OSIRIS-REx mission. Each catalog features geospatial maps with comparative visualizations across all candidate sites. These catalogs are decisional products, and as such are historical documents. They are not final curated science products.
