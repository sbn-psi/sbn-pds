Overview

  • Abstract: This bundle contains all reported timings of observed asteroid, planet, and planetary satellite occultation events, as well as axes derived from those timings by David Herald. Most of these timings are unpublished and have been collected by Eric Frappa, Tsutomu Hayamizu, Steve Kerr and Brad Timerson, and collated by Herald. This version is complete through to the end of 2017.
