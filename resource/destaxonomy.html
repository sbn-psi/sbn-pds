Taxonomy, colors, and slope parameters for asteroids from the Dark Energy Survey Bundle V1.0

This bundle provides taxonomical information from Dark Energy Survey (DES) data for 16517 asteroids for which gri slope and i-z colors are available, taxonomical complex information for 58116 asteroids with DES colors g-r, g-i, and a list of 409 new possible V-type objects.

Release Date: 2024-04-05
