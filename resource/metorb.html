Steel Meteoroid Orbits

This data set contains meteoroid orbits from photographic, TV system, and radar meteoroid surveys collected from the International Astronomical Union Meteor Data Center (IAU MDC) by Duncan Steel and reviewed and discussed in Steel (1996). The data cover the time period 1936-1983.
