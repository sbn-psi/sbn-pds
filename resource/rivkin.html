Rivkin 3-Micron Data

This data set includes the 3-micron data on asteroids by Andy Rivkin and collaborators, published through 2002. Also included are their published spectra of Phobos and Deimos.

Use the following citation to reference this data set:

"Rivkin, Andrew S. and Carol Neese, Eds. Rivkin Three Micron Asteroid Data V3.0. EAR-A-3-RDR-RIVKIN-THREE-MICRON-V3.0, NASA Planetary Data System, 2003."
