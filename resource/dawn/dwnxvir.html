Dawn Cruise - VIR

Status - Archived

These data sets contain the raw and calibrated Dawn VIR IR and VIS data from the cruise phase from launch to Vesta (Sep 2009-May 2011), and Vesta to Ceres (Sep 2012-Dec 2014).

VIR IR L1a (raw), all Cruise Browse Download (377 Mb)
VIR VIS L1a (raw), all Cruise Browse Download (172 Mb)
VIR IR L1b (calibrated), all Cruise Browse Download (419 Mb)
VIR VIS L1b (calibrated), all Cruise Browse Download (700 Mb)
