Dawn at Vesta - Framing Camera

The Dawn spacecraft visited the asteroid 4 Vesta for the time period May 3, 2011 to Sept. 5, 2012. Framing Camera 2 is one of two identical units flying on the Dawn spacecraft, and the only one acquiring science data at Vesta. These two data sets include raw (level 1a) and calibrated (level 1b) versions of all available images acquired during the Vesta Encounter.
Data Set Info

Calibration data are available in a separate data set, available here.

Browse and search the PDS Dawn Vesta archive using the Small Bodies Image Browser.
