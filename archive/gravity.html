Gravity Models

These data sets contain gravity models which are a detailed model of the gravitational field of an object, such as spherical harmonics, radial gravity acceleration, or internal density. Gravity models are derived from radio science data which make use of the radio telemetry signal between the spacecraft and earth to accurately determine the position of the spacecraft

  • Dawn Vesta Gravity Models
  • Dawn Ceres Gravity Models
  • NEAR Collected Shape and Gravity Models - contains shape and gravity models of NEAR targets Mathilde and Eros
