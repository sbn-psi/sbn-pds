Galileo Orbiter Data Archive

Main target body: Jupiter
Other target bodies: Dust, and asteroids Ida, Gaspra, and Dactyl
Launch date: 18 October 1989
951 Gaspra encounter: 29 October 1991
243 Ida and Dactyl encounter: 28 August 1993
Jupiter orbit insertion: 8 December 1995
End of mission: 21 September 2003

Galileo Orbiter primary instrument data at SBN:

Data type Browse/Download Description
Dust Galileo Dust Detector Subsystem (GDDS) GDDS can detect particles having masses between 10-16g
and 10-6g traveling at relative speeds of up to
70 km/s. GDDS has detected interplanetary dust from asteroids
and comets, interstellar dust, and streams of dust originating
from Io volcanic activity.
Images Ida/Gaspra Collection A collection of Galileo and other data specific to Ida
and Gaspra, selected from the full data sets.
