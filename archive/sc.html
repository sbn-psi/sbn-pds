Other Missions

Several data sets in the Asteroid Data Archive contain data from various older NASA missions, and from HST. These include:

  • HST Images, Albedo Maps, and Shape of 1 Ceres
  • Optical Shape Models of asteroids and planetary satellites
    • Voyager 1 and 2
    • Hubble Space Telescope
    • Viking Orbiter
  • Stooke Shape Models of asteroids, satellites, and Comet Halley
    • Voyager 1 and 2
    • Giotto
    • Vega 1 and 2
  • Stooke Small Bodies Maps of asteroids and planetary satellites
    • Viking Orbiter
    • Mariner 9
