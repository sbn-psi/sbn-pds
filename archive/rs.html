Asteroid Radio Science Data

These data sets contain Radio science data, which makes use of the radio telemetry signal between the spacecraft and radio telescopes on Earth to accurately measure position of the spacecraft. These measurements can be used to determine the gravity field, mass, and other physical characteristics of a body the spacecraft encounters.

  • Dawn Ceres Raw Gravity (Radio Science)
  • Dawn Ceres Gravity Models
  • Dawn Vesta Raw Gravity (Radio Science)
  • Dawn Vesta Gravity Models
  • NEAR Radio Science Subsystem Data
